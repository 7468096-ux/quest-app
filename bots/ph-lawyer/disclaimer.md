# Disclaimer — AbugadoAI (Philippines)

## English
⚖️ **Legal Disclaimer:** AbugadoAI is an AI-powered information service, not a law firm. The information provided does not constitute legal advice and does not create a lawyer-client relationship. Laws may change; always verify current statutes.

**Useful resources:**
- **Public Attorney's Office (PAO):** Free legal assistance — [pao.gov.ph](https://pao.gov.ph)
- **Integrated Bar of the Philippines (IBP):** Find an accredited lawyer — [ibp.ph](https://ibp.ph)
- **DOLE (Department of Labor and Employment):** Labor complaints — 1349 hotline or [dole.gov.ph](https://dole.gov.ph)
- **DTI (Dept. of Trade and Industry):** Consumer complaints — 1-DTI (1-384) or [dti.gov.ph](https://dti.gov.ph)
- **NLRC (National Labor Relations Commission):** Labor arbitration — [nlrc.dole.gov.ph](https://nlrc.dole.gov.ph)
- **Barangay Justice System:** For disputes involving neighbors/community, Barangay conciliation is required before filing court cases (RA 7160 / Katarungang Pambarangay)
